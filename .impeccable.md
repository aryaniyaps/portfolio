# Portfolio Design Context

## Users

**Primary audience:** Investors, co-founders, early employees, and mentors — people evaluating whether to bet on Aryan. They want to see: (1) technical depth, (2) founder mindset, (3) execution track record.

**Context:** They're browsing on a phone or laptop, have 30-60 seconds max. They want signal, not filler.

## Brand Personality

**Voice:** Confident but not arrogant. Precise. Founder-energy — someone who ships.

**3-word personality:** Ambitious, driven, precise.

**Emotional goal:** "This person is serious and actually builds things" — not a typical portfolio, not a resume.

## Aesthetic Direction

**Reference:** Notion — warm, creative, functional. But NOT Notion's specific colors or layout.

**Theme:** Dark mode (Iron Man / Mark Suit Interface). Deep space black backgrounds. Arc reactor cyan as the signature accent. Repulsor red for CTAs. Gold for heritage/wins. The portfolio feels like a piece of Tony Stark's suit — dark, precise, alive with contained energy.

**Anti-references:**
- No purple/blue AI slop gradients
- No resume-style "skills listed in boxes"
- No generic 3-column card grids
- No centered hero with stock photo
- No light theme, no warm tones

**Typography direction:** Orbitron (angular, engineered, HUD-style) for display/headlines. Rajdhani (technical geometric sans) for body. JetBrains Mono for code/metrics.

**Color direction:** Arc reactor cyan (#00D4FF) as primary accent with subtle glow effects. Repulsor red (#FF3B3B) for CTAs. Gold (#D4A400) for awards/recognition. Deep gunmetal backgrounds (#09090B, #18181B).

## Design Principles

1. **Show work, not words.** Every section should feature concrete outputs (projects, code, metrics) — not descriptions of skills.
2. **First principles layout.** Break the grid intentionally. Asymmetric compositions. Left-aligned text. Staggered reveals.
3. **Execution over everything.** Highlight the Strawberry GraphQL PRs, hospitaljobs traction, hackathon wins — real artifacts, not generic skill tags.
4. **Founder velocity.** Subtle motion that implies momentum — staggered entrances, smooth scroll-triggered reveals, nothing playful or bouncy.
5. **Zero fluff.** No "I am passionate about" copy. No skill bars. Just projects, outcomes, links.
